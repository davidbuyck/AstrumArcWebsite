About AstrumArc LLC

AstrumArc LLC collaborates closely with the University of Minnesota, leading medical device companies, and an international team of medical advisors to develop innovative educational and entertainment simulators, including Cardiac Pro MD. By integrating academic research, clinical expertise, global insights, and advanced technologies, we create precise, engaging applications designed to elevate medical education, patient care, preoperative planning, and surgical assistance worldwide:

Cardiac Pro MD

Cardiac Pro MD is a comprehensive suite of interactive educational and training simulators designed to enhance global cardiac care by providing realistic simulations, interactive anatomy models, and intuitive clinical reference tools. Available on mobile, desktop, web, and XR platforms, it supports healthcare professionals, students, and patients worldwide.

Medical Mobile Apps

Educational tools featuring 3D heart scans: "A Heart to Learn," "UMN TEE Simulator," "UMN AR Heart," "UMN Stents," "UMN TTE Simulator," "Coronary Quest."

Medical VR Simulators

XR simulations for Meta Quest and PC VR: TEE, TTE, LBBP pacing, Amplatzer occluder, UMN VR Heart, CHD 360.

Medical Web Applications

Interactive WebGL/WebXR experiences, including UMN TEE Simulator.

Preoperative Planning & Surgical Assistance

Advanced simulation and visualization technologies designed to support surgical teams during preoperative planning and intraoperative decision-making, enhancing procedural accuracy and patient safety.

Universe Simulator

Interstellar exploration using NASA’s star database.

Heaven's Ladder

Ascend towering platforms, execute breathtaking leaps of faith, and navigate the narrowest of paths with precision.

AI & Cloud Integration

OpenAI NLP, Azure Cognitive Services for speech, secure Azure Storage, integrated for both educational purposes and real-time operative assistance.
